Restoring hope, changing lives.

UltraGC Foundation supports education, nutrition and protection for vulnerable people — scholarships, widow feeding, orphan care and rescue for abused girls. We partner with communities to build lasting change.

Scholarships

Widow Feeding
Food packs and community care so widows and their families don't go hungry.
Orphan Support
Medical, educational and emotional support for orphans in our programs.
Girl Child Protection
Rescue, rehabilitation and mentoring for abused girls to restore dignity and safety.
Our Approach
We work directly with communities, schools and local leaders to identify needs. Our programs are built on dignity, transparency and impact. Donations are channelled carefully to maximize benefit for beneficiaries.

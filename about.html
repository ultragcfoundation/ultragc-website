About UltraGC Foundation

Mission

To uplift vulnerable communities through education, nutrition and protection.

Vision

A world where every child has access to education and every widow is cared for.

Values

Compassion • Dignity • Transparency • Community-led Action

Who we help

We focus on less privileged students, widows, orphans and girls who have experienced abuse. Our interventions include scholarships, feeding programs, sponsorship, mentorship and rescue referrals where necessary.
